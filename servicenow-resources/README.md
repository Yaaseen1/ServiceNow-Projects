# ServiceNow Resources

Welcome to my **ServiceNow Resources** directory! 

I created this space as I continue my own journey in the ServiceNow ecosystem. My goal here is twofold: to document what I am learning as I build custom applications and study for certifications, and to share those insights so others can benefit.

I strongly believe that none of us are on this journey alone. There will always be people who can help guide us, and there will always be people we can help in return. As I learn, I want to pay it forward.

## What to Expect Here

This directory is just getting started, but as it grows, you will find:

*   **`/sn-developer-toolkit`** *(Coming soon)*: A dedicated space for reusable scripts, configurations, and actionable code from my Personal Developer Instance.
*   **Documentation & Tips**: Real-world lessons, architecture notes, and troubleshooting guides bridging the gap between business analysis and technical implementation.
*   **Shared Knowledge**: Anything I discover that might help another analyst or developer level up their skills.

Whether you are here to grab a quick code snippet or looking for inspiration for your own builds, I hope you find something useful. Feel free to explore, use what helps you, and keep learning. We all grow better together.
