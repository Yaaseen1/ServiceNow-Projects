### 📂 Archived: Initial Progress & Iterations
This directory is a personal "time capsule" for this project. While most portfolios only show the polished final product, I chose to keep these early screenshots because they represent the real learning process I went through while building this application.

Why these are here:
Continuous Improvement: When I first started, I realized my forms were missing critical fields like "Opened by" and "Work Notes." Instead of just ignoring those gaps, I used them as a pivot point to go back into the table schema and form layout to ensure the app met professional standards for auditability.

A Reflection of Growth: Keeping these allows me to reflect on who I was at the start of this project vs. who I am now. It’s easy to forget the "Aha!" moments once you've mastered a tool, but these images remind me of the specific technical hurdles I cleared in Flow Designer and Table Builder.

The Power of Iteration: I kept updating and refining until I was satisfied. This archive serves as proof that I don’t just build to "get it done"—I build to improve, and I’m proud of the distance between these early drafts and the live, automated solution I eventually deployed.
